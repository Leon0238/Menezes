ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm 21 years old, brazilian and since I was a kid I was passionate for art and technology, and like John Lasseter once said: "The art challenges the technology, and the technology inspires the art."

In 2013 I didn't know anything about "making art on computer" so I started to study at Melies (São Paulo) to learn more about graphic design.

After learning more about graphic design at Melies, I started to study at AnimSchool. An online school which the instructors are animators from the greatest animation studios of the world, like Disney Animation Studios, BlueSky, Reel FX and others.

And in 2015 I started to work as a system developer at "Consultoria FR" where I can improve my HTML, CSS, JavaScript (and jQuery), Ajax, VB.NET and SQL skills.

That's me, and what I most love to do is create and create. It doesn't matter if it is a web system, web page or even an animation scene.

Leonardo Menezes
